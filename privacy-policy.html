Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Eldero+. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy governs the privacy practices of Eldero+ regarding our 30-Day Golden Wellness Challenge, expert consultation services, health tracking tools, and our website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering for the 30-Day Golden Wellness Challenge, requesting consultations, or communicating with us. This includes:

  • Personal Details: Full name, phone number, and age range.
  • Health & Wellness Data: Daily wellness tracking data (e.g., yoga activities, meditation logs, hydration logs, sleep logs, and meals) that you track through the app.
  • Communication Data: Any information you share when contacting our experts or support team.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and provide the features of our wellness programs, including:

  • Setting up and managing your customized 30-day wellness plan.
  • Sending automated reminders and notifications to keep you on track.
  • Facilitating consultations with experts in fitness, psychology, diet, and nutrition.
  • Processing registration and one-time payments for our wellness package (4,000 INR).
  • Improving our services, customer support, and user experience.

4. Data Storage and Local-First Approach

To prioritize accessibility and data privacy, much of your day-to-day progress logging, tracking data, and configuration parameters are stored locally on your device. We only sync or transmit personal registration details and consultation inquiries to our systems to facilitate services and support.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share information with third parties under the following circumstances:

  • Consultants and Experts: With your consent, relevant details are shared with our partner experts in fitness, psychology, diet, and nutrition to facilitate personalized consultations.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party providers who perform payment processing, notification delivery, or hosting services on our behalf.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or to protect our rights, safety, and security.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable organizational, technical, and administrative measures to protect personal information within our organization. However, please remember that no transmission over the internet or data storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information. Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold about you. You can also disable browser storage or notifications at any time to halt data collection.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or regulations. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last updated. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
